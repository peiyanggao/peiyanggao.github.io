I am a Ph.D. student in Neurology at [Capital Medical University](https://www.ccmu.edu.cn/), working under the supervision of [Prof. Yi Tang](https://neurology.xwhosp.com.cn/Html/Hospitals/Doctors/Index45.html) at [Xuanwu Hospital](https://www.xwhosp.com.cn/). I received my Master degree in Neurology from [Qingdao University](https://www.qdu.edu.cn/) in 2024, supervised by [Prof. Jin-Tai Yu](https://www.huashan.org.cn/zhuanjia/doctorinfo/3190.html).

My research centers on the **epidemiology and molecular mechanisms of dementia and cognitive disorders**. By integrating large-scale population cohorts (UK Biobank, CABLE, ADNI, ROSMAP) with multi-omics data and experimental validation, I aim to identify modifiable risk factors and early biomarkers of Alzheimer's disease, and to elucidate the biological pathways through which they contribute to cognitive decline.

Research Interests
======
- Epidemiology of dementia and cognitive impairment
- Modifiable risk factors of dementia (frailty, liver dysfunction, chronic pain, social determinants)
- Multi-omics biomarkers: proteomics, metabolomics, and genomics
- Single-cell transcriptomics of neurodegenerative diseases
